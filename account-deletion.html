Delete your Fable Forge account

Deleting your account is permanent and immediate. This action cannot be undone.

In‑app deletion steps

  1. Open Fable Forge and log in to your account.
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom.
  3. Select Account Settings.
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What happens when you delete

  • Your personal data and game content stored by Fable Forge are permanently deleted without delay.
  • There is no recovery or cooldown period.
  • We do not keep backups.
  • Published content: if others have already viewed, copied, or shared your published games, those external copies may persist outside our control.
  • Third‑party service providers (e.g., AI model APIs) may retain minimal logs for a limited time per their own policies.
